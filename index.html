Why The Mental Sideline Exists

Athletes are taught to push through pain, perform under pressure, and never show weakness. But no one talks about what happens on the inside.

The Mental Sideline is a community and resource platform built by a student athlete who gets it -- to make mental health conversations as normal as talking about your game.

Changing the Culture
